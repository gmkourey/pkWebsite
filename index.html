About Paul & Katie

Katie and Paul have both been with Coldwell Banker for more than 27 years, all in the San Ramon Valley. They excelled right from the start (being named Coldwell Banker’s “Rookie of The Year” for Contra Costa County in 1993) and have consistently been among the company’s top 100 producers in the entire Bay Area. If you’re looking for stability, integrity, synergy and professionalism, you’ve found your Realtors.

Katie & Paul have four children, all of whom attended schools in the highly sought-after San Ramon Valley Unified School District. We have a vested interest in the San Ramon Valley community. Having lived and worked here for more than 30 years, we have an extensive knowledge of the community to go with our abundance of real estate experience.

In the spirit of giving back to the community, Katie and Paul have been team leaders in the San Ramon Valley Fire Protection District’s “CERT” (Community Emergency Response Team) program for almost a decade. It’s all about keeping our neighborhoods safe and helping families be as prepared as possible if and when a disaster strikes.
